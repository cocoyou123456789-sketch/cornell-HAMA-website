Cornell Hospitality Asset Manager Association

Building the next generation of hospitality investment, ownership, and asset management leaders.

About Us

Cornell HAMA brings together students interested in the business of lodging assets, from acquisition underwriting and owner relations to revenue strategy, brand positioning, capital planning, and disposition.

Recruitment

Members receive curated opportunities across hotel ownership groups, REITs, private equity platforms, advisory firms, and asset management teams.

Speaker Events

Conversations with hospitality owners, asset managers, investors, and operators introduce students to the decisions behind hotel value.

Industry Chats

HAMA hosts alumni and industry guests for practical conversations on market cycles, capital decisions, renovation planning, brand standards, and owner-operator alignment.

Career Treks

Select members visit hospitality companies and real estate investors to understand how ownership decisions are made in New York, Miami, Los Angeles, and other hotel markets.

Speaker Events

Hospitality Asset Management Conversations

Students learn directly from practitioners through talks, Q&A sessions, and informal discussions on operating performance, capital planning, and ownership strategy.

Global Network

Connected to the Hospitality Asset Management Community

HAMA is recognized across the hospitality industry as a professional network for hotel asset managers. Cornell HAMA gives students a campus home for that same discipline: ownership thinking, operating performance, capital strategy, and long-term asset value.
