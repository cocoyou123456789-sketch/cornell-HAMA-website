Cornell Hospitality Asset Manager Association

Building the next generation of hospitality investment, ownership, and asset management leaders.

What We Do

Cornell HAMA brings together students interested in the business of lodging assets, from acquisition underwriting and owner relations to revenue strategy, brand positioning, capital planning, and disposition.

Recruitment

Members receive curated opportunities across hotel ownership groups, REITs, private equity platforms, advisory firms, and asset management teams.

Programs

Semester programming introduces hotel valuation, operating statements, benchmarking, management contracts, franchise agreements, and asset strategy through applied workshops.

Speaker Series

HAMA hosts executives, alumni, and operators for conversations on market cycles, capital decisions, renovation planning, brand standards, and owner-operator alignment.

Career Treks

Select members visit hospitality companies and real estate investors to understand how ownership decisions are made in New York, Miami, Los Angeles, and other hotel markets.

Experiential Learning

Hotel Asset Management Intensive

Students work through live-style hospitality cases, evaluating operating performance, competitive sets, capex priorities, lender considerations, and the owner memo that turns analysis into action.
